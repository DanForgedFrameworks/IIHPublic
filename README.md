# IIH UK Resource Library

Patient information leaflets, clinical resources, and digital assets produced by **IIH UK** for people living with Idiopathic Intracranial Hypertension, their families and carers, and the healthcare professionals who support them.

**IIH UK** is a registered charity in England & Wales (1143522) and Scotland (SC043294).  
Website: [www.iih.org.uk](https://www.iih.org.uk)

---

## What is IIH?

Idiopathic Intracranial Hypertension (IIH) is a rare neurological condition caused by raised pressure around the brain and spinal cord. It predominantly affects women of childbearing age and, if untreated, can cause permanent sight loss. IIH UK supports patients, families, carers, and clinicians with evidence-based information and resources.

## Accessibility

All digital resources in this library are produced to meet:
- **WCAG 2.2 Level AA** (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines)
- **EN 301 549** (European accessibility standard for ICT products)

Interactive HTML assets are fully self-contained single files with no external dependencies — they work offline and on any device.
